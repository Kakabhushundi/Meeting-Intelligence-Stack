# Meeting Intelligence Stack

> Six specialist prompts for turning meeting transcripts into organizational capital.

Most meeting notes die in someone's Notion. This stack extracts the actual value — decisions, expertise, risk signals, team dynamics — and turns it into something you can act on, reference later, or build systems from.

---

## Prompts at a Glance

| Prompt | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **1 — Tacit Knowledge Extractor** | Finds the unwritten rules and expertise hiding in conversation |
| **2 — Decision Journal Builder** | Logs what was decided, why, and what you didn't know yet |
| **3 — Team Dynamics Detector** | Surfaces participation gaps, influence patterns, conflict signals |
| **4 — Pre-Mortem Analyst** | Imagines the failure and works backward to prevent it |
| **5 — Knowledge Asset Creator** | Converts insights into searchable, reusable reference objects |
| **6 — After-Action Review Generator** | Extracts lessons that actually change future behavior |

---

## How to Use

1. Copy the prompt text from `SKILL.md`
2. Paste into Claude (or your AI of choice)
3. Replace the bracketed `[INPUTS]` with your transcript and context
4. Run — or chain multiple prompts on the same transcript

See `SKILL.md` for full prompt text, recommended pairings, and integration notes.

---

## Works Well With

- **Fireflies.ai** — auto-transcription → paste output here
- **Granola** — structured meeting notes → paste output here  
- **Zoom / Teams transcripts** — export → paste here
- **Notion** — store Knowledge Assets (Prompt 5) in a searchable database
- **SharePoint** — store Decision Logs (Prompt 2) for audit trail
